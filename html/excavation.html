Learn More about Excavation and Sitework Services

From cut and fill to curb and sidewalk, Jones Contracting, Inc has provided site work for many housing developments and buildings. Our main goal is to provide comprehensive site work service with as little subcontracting as possible. Our site-work services include:

  • Land Clearing
  • Cut and Fill
  • Blasting
  • Utility Installation
  • Road Construction
  • Standard or Sloped Concrete, Asphalt, and Granite Curbing
  • Asphalt Roadway Paving
  • Asphalt and Concrete Sidewalks with ADA Handicap Ramps
  • Foundation Excavation and Backfill
  • Temporary Bridge Construction
  • Concrete Bridge and Culvert Construction

We also have much experience with Stormwater Best Management Practices including:

  • Retention and Detention Ponds
  • Bio-retention Ponds
  • Rain Gardens
  • Bioswales
  • On-site Groundwater Recharge Systems
